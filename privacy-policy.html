Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

Introduction

Stephane's Dev Tools ("we", "us", or "our") operates an automated notification service that sends SMS messages via Twilio. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with this messaging service.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to deliver SMS notifications:

  • Phone numbers of message recipients
  • Message content and delivery metadata (e.g., timestamps, delivery status)

How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of sending automated development notifications and alerts to the account owner. Messages are triggered by internal development tools and workflows.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Phone numbers and message data are transmitted through Twilio's messaging platform solely for the purpose of delivering SMS notifications. No data is shared with any other third party.

Data Retention

Message logs and phone numbers are retained only as long as necessary for the operation of the notification service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we handle, including the use of encrypted API connections and secure credential storage.

Your Rights

You may opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may also contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
